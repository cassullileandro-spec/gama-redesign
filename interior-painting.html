(The process)

How we work

( 1 ) Taping & covering

Hardware, floors and fixtures are protected before a single can of paint is opened.

( 2 ) Wall preparation

Cracks and imperfections are filled with plaster compound until the surface reads as new.

( 3 ) Sanding

Every patched area is sanded perfectly smooth — the finish is only as good as what's beneath it.

( 4 ) Cleaning

All dust is removed so nothing stands between the wall and the paint.

( 5 ) Priming & sealing

Applied whenever the surface, stains or a color change call for it.

( 6 ) Painting

Brushes for the detail work, rollers for the broad planes — even coverage, crisp lines.

( 7 ) Final walkthrough

We review every room with you against our quality standards before we leave.
