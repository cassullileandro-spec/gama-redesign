(Overview)

Wood, elevated to art.

From built-ins and doors to furniture and paneling, we restore and refinish wood with techniques that honor the material — stains, lacquers, glazes and hand-finished details.

(The process)

How we work

( 1 ) Vision & consult

We sit with you to define the look — tone, sheen, texture, character.

( 2 ) Preparation

Careful disassembly where needed, deep cleaning and masking.

( 3 ) Sanding & repair

Surfaces are restored, imperfections corrected, grain revealed.

( 4 ) Custom finish

Stain, lacquer or paint applied in controlled, patient layers.

( 5 ) Protection & delivery

Clear protective coats, reassembly, and a final review with you.
